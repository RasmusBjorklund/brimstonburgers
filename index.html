History

Back in 1940, Christoffer Brimston opened the doors for what would become a staple in American burger resturants. With the goal of becoming a simple resturant that served fresh burger for a low price, success started showing early for Brimston Burgers. After one year Brimston Burgers had become so successful so Christoffer decided to open another Brimston resturant in the south part of New York. Today, the same doors that Brimston opened 79 years ago are still standing open, and and Brimston burgers still have the motto that started it. Fresh burgers for a low price.
- Christoffer Brimston
